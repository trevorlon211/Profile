About This Guy!

Who am I?

Trevorlon is a fullstack web developer based in New York.Prior to pursuing his dream career, Trevorlon worked in Accounting as an account receivable manager for a travel reception company. Despite his work history he decided it was time for him to take a chance in what really sparked his interest. From a early age he was drawn to music and the creative side of life. Finally taking a leap of faith he pursued his career in web development. Trevorlon has achieved certification in full stack web development and has since created multi page, fully responsive websites. He enjoys creating clean, dynamic, user-friendly websites while fulfilling today's web standards. When he is not knee deep in his projects and keeping up with current web development practices, Trevorlon enjoys spending time with his family, friends and volunteering playing guitar for Hillsongnyc.
